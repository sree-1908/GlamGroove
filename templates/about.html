About Glam Groove

Glam Groove is your one-stop destination for premium skincare and beauty products curated to elevate your self-care routine. Our mission is to deliver high-quality, sustainable, and cruelty-free products that empower you to look and feel your best every day.

SHIPPING

All orders are shipped from our main warehouse in Bengaluru. Orders placed before 10 pm IST/ will be processed and shipped by the next business day.

We offer international shipping via trusted partners. Customs duties and taxes are calculated at checkout. Transit time varies between 4 to 12 business days. Please note we do not accept returns on international orders.

We currently do not ship to P.O. boxes, APO/FPO addresses, or certain remote areas.

RETURNS

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unused and in original packaging.

Contact our support team to initiate a return. We will provide you with a return shipping label and instructions.

Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after we receive your return. Original shipping fees are non-refundable.
