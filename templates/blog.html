7 Skincare Habits to Break Now

Great skincare is a long game—little steps made nightly keep your skin at its best.

  • 1. Skipping sunscreen: Even indoors, UV damage happens. Use SPF daily.
  • 2. Over-exfoliating: It strips your skin barrier. Twice a week is plenty.
  • 3. Not removing makeup: Sleeping in makeup clogs pores and ages skin faster.
  • 4. Ignoring your neck: Your neck needs love too—apply serums and sunscreen!
  • 5. Touching your face constantly: This spreads bacteria and oils.
  • 6. Using dirty pillowcases: Wash them weekly to avoid breakouts.
  • 7. Not hydrating enough: Drink water and use a good moisturizer daily.

Small changes go a long way. Break these habits and your skin will thank you in just a few weeks.
